Physics Questions

Kinematics: What is displacement?

Displaement is the change in position relative to starting position.



Dynamics: What direction does centripetal force act relative to an object?

Centripetal force acts perpendicular to an object's velocity.



Momentum and Energy: What is an elastic collision?

An elastic collision is a collision in which there is no net loss in kinetic energy in the system as a result of the collision.
